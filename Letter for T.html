My dearest Tvisha,

There are a thousand things I want to say to you, and yet every time I look at you, words seem too small for what I feel. So I wrote this — just for you — because you deserve to know how deeply, completely, and endlessly you are loved.

You walked into my life and quietly rearranged everything — in the most beautiful way. The way you laugh, the way you care, the way you make even the ordinary moments feel like something worth remembering — that is you. That is the magic you carry.

I fall for you a little more every single day. Not just in the grand moments, but in the quiet ones too — in the silences, in the small gestures, in the way you make me feel at home wherever we are.

You are my favorite person. My safe place. My greatest adventure. And loving you, Tvisha, is the best thing I have ever done.

This is me, telling you — I lOVE YOU. More than these words can hold. More than you might know. But I will spend every day making sure you do.

Forever yours,

Vaibhav
